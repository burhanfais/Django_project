Beneli TRK 251

Benelli TRK 251 is a adventure bike available in only 1 variant and 3 colours. TRK 251 price starts at Rs. 2,99,000 in India. The Benelli TRK 251 is powered by 249cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 25.47 bhp and a torque of 21.1 Nm. With both front and rear disc brakes, Benelli TRK 251 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This TRK 251 bike weighs 164 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 18 liters.
